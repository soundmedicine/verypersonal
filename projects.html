Thrive!

This is an app I built in collaboration with research about high-altitude growth and development from the Ebert Family Clinic in Frisco, Colorado. I learned and built in Angular within a week for a capstone presentation as part of Galvanize's six-month web development immersive. It allows birth and growth rates to be charted and compared to high-altitude standards, versus sea-level standards.

Tech: Angular 6, NodeJS, Express, ChartJS, HTML, Materialize CSS, Knex, PostgreSQL
